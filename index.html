Temple Jewellery

Temple jewellery refers to those pieces of jewellery that Bharatnatyam dancers wear. The inspiration for this jewellery comes from the ornaments and pieces of jewellery that one uses for decking up the South Indian idols of Hindu goddesses and gods.
